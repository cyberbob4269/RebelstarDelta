# Player fantasy

You are a raider on the **Moon**. Storm **Moonbase Delta**, destroy **ISAAC**, survive.

**Feel:** vacuum outside, hard sun, stars and a huge Milky Way, Earthrise over a crater rim. The base looks **mostly finished**, built with **regolith blocks** mined by robots — near-future realism, not plastic sci-fi.

**Good ending:** fly out and see the whole Moon in a sea of stars.
